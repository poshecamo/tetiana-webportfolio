About Me

I am Tetiana Moshenets, a professional sports ballroom dance instructor, choreographer, and fitness trainer with over 20 years of international teaching experience. I am based in Vancouver and passionate about helping students of all ages master the art of dance with confidence and joy. I have worked across Ukraine, the United Arab Emirates, and Canada, preparing dancers for competitions, choreographing performances, and teaching both beginners and advanced students. I specialize in ballroom dance, which consists of two programs: Standard (waltz, tango, foxtrot, Viennese waltz, quickstep) and Latin (cha-cha-cha, samba, rumba, paso doble, jive). My approach is based on building strong technique, musicality, and expression. I offer private lessons, group classes, choreography coaching, ProAm training, and fitness programs focused on flexibility and posture development.

I hold a Master’s Degree in Choreography from European University and Bachelor’s and Specialist Degrees in Choreography and Organization Management from Kyiv National University of Culture and Arts. I am also a certified sports ballroom dance competition judge.

I provide professional ballroom and Latin dance training, choreography coaching, ProAm program lessons, fitness and stretching classes, and competition preparation for students of all ages and levels.

Dance Classes Offered

Ballet

I lead foundational classical choreography lessons designed to develop flexibility, strength, endurance, and proper posture. These sessions enhance coordination and fluidity, supporting overall physical development and graceful movement.

Fitness Coach

I conduct functional fitness training sessions, enhanced with dance movements to develop physical fitness, flexibility, and inner energy. It’s important to me not just to train the body but also to inspire each person toward positive change. I organize fitness marathons where participants document their journey with “before” and “after” photos, allowing them to see real results and feel proud of their progress.

Ballroom

I teach children and adults sports ballroom dancing, helping each person discover their grace, strength, and confidence. In my classes, we don’t just learn the steps — we cultivate character, a sense of rhythm, and a love of dance. I believe that dance opens new opportunities not only on the dance floor but in life itself.
